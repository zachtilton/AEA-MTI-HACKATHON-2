About

What is this?

The AEA/MTI Hackathon 2 is a low-barrier, fully virtual event designed for evaluation practitioners at every level. Whether you want to stress-test an AI-generated claim, build a practical AI tool for your workflow, or remix a shared template alongside colleagues — there's a path for you.

No coding background required. No prize competition—but there are five $100 lottery tickets up for grabs for those who submit an entry. Just take an hour (or less) of focused exploration, a short reflection, and a spot in our public gallery of practitioner work.

This event is co-hosted by the American Evaluation Association (AEA) and MTI/MERL Tech, and is part of an ongoing community effort to think critically and creatively about AI in the evaluation field.
