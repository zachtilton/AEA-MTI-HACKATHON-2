Collab Template Library

Ten community-built templates ready to open, use, and remix. Pick one that fits your work, improve it for your context, and publish your version.

🤝 How this works

  1. Click Open template to open the artifact in Claude.
  2. Use it for a few minutes on a real or hypothetical task.
  3. Identify 1–3 things you’d change for your context or population.
  4. To make your changes: Right-click the Customize button (top right of the artifact) and select “Copy link.” Paste the link into a new browser tab and press Enter — this opens an editable Claude conversation with the template pre-loaded. Type your changes directly into the chat.
  5. Publish your remix by clicking the Share button (↗) in the artifact panel to get a public link.
  6. Submit your remix with a brief change-log.

⚠️ Note: Clicking Customize directly may not work in all browsers. Right-click → copy link → paste in a new tab is the most reliable approach.
